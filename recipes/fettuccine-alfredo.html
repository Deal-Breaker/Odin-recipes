Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Fettuccine alfredo with charred chicken and broccoli, cooked in a light sauce comprised of chicken broth and heavy whipping cream. A delicious dinner that anyone can make, and is sure to put a smile on your family's face!

Ingredients

  • 1 large boneless chicken breast
  • 2 gloves of garlic, minced
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 egg yolk
  • salt to taste
  • 1/2 a pound of fettuccine
  • 1/2 fresh chopped broccoli
  • 1 cup freshy grated Parmigian-Reggiano cheese, divided

Steps

  1. Combine chicken breasts and chicken broth in a saucepan over medium-high heat.
